Fathalla Coding & Programming Contest (FCPC)

Welcome to the FCPC

The FCPC is a competitive programming event organized for the students of Fathalla International School of Applied Technology. The contest is designed to enhance programming skills and problem-solving abilities, focusing on C++. Participants will tackle a series of algorithmic challenges that test their logic, coding speed, and efficiency under time constraints.

Winners will receive prizes, certificates, and recognition for their exceptional skills in programming.

Note: The form will close on December 20th at 12:00 AM (midnight). Make sure to apply before the deadline!
